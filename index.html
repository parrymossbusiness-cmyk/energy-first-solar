Helpful answers

Before you
go solar.

Quick answers for the questions homeowners ask before requesting a quote.

How do I know if my home is a good fit for solar?

A solar assessment reviews roof condition, sun exposure, shade, energy usage, and utility rules to determine whether solar makes sense.

Do you offer battery backup?

Yes. Battery backup can be discussed as part of the design for homeowners who want stored energy and added resilience.

Will solar eliminate my electric bill?

Solar savings depend on system size, energy use, utility rates, roof orientation, and financing. The estimate should explain realistic expectations.

How long does solar installation take?

The physical installation may be quick, but total timelines depend on design, permits, utility approval, inspection, and interconnection.
